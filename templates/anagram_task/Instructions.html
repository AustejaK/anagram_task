Anagram task

In this task we will ask you to solve {{Constants.total_number}} anagrams. Each correct solution is worth {{Constants.bonus}}. You will have 5 minutes to complete the task. When time passes, the task ends automatically. The more anagrams you solve, the higher is your bonus payment.

On the next page you will see a table with numbered tabs. Click on each tab to reveal the anagram. It does not matter if you type in capital or lowercase letters. After typing in your answer, click the button "Mark as finished". In this way you can see how many anagrams have been finished and how many are still left to do. If you don't know the answer or don't want to solve that anagram, simply leave the field empty and click on the next numbered tab.

All the anagrams are solvable. The words are in a singular or infinitive form. At the end of the study you will be given the solution to all the anagrams and will be told how many anagrams you solved correctly.

When you are ready to proceed, please click "Next". Be aware that by clicking "Next", the time countdown begins. The task will finish automatically after time has passed and you will move to the next page.
